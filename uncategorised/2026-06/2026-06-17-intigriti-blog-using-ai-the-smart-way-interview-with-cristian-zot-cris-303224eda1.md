## Defensive takeaways
- Always provide AI tools with explicit scope definitions to prevent out-of-scope submissions and wasted effort
- Use AI as a reasoning and learning aid rather than autonomous vulnerability discovery engine
- Maintain human judgment in the loop - verify AI suggestions independently before proceeding
- Leverage AI for conceptual learning through visual explanations and error code interpretation
- Use AI for report refinement and conciseness only after independent verification of findings
- Frame AI interactions around specific vulnerability classes or testing objectives for better results
- Require foundational security experience (months of practice) before effectively incorporating AI into workflows
