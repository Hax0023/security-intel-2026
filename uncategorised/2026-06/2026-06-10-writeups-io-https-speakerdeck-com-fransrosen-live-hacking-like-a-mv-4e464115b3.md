## Attacker mindset
Systematic competitor focused on efficient vulnerability discovery. Emphasizes methodology over luck, strategic target selection, understanding application architecture, and continuous learning. Mindset balances depth of research with breadth across multiple targets to maximize impact and earnings.

## Defensive takeaways
- Implement comprehensive security testing across all applications and modern web technologies
- Establish mature bug bounty programs to attract elite researchers and discover critical issues
- Monitor and patch vulnerabilities across interconnected systems and dependencies
- Develop security awareness among development teams about common attack vectors
- Create layered defenses that make exploitation of chained vulnerabilities difficult
- Maintain active threat intelligence on emerging exploitation techniques
